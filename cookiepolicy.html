Cookie Policy

Understanding Cookies and Your Privacy

At PowerBall, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to maintaining transparency about the way we collect and use data. This Cookie Policy is designed to explain what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices you have regarding their use while visiting our website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

  • Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They contain information about your browsing behavior and help improve your online experience.

Why Do We Use Cookies?

  • Cookies help us enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences, personalizing content, and making navigation smoother.

How We Use Cookies:

  • We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To remember your preferences, such as language settings and login details, for future visits, providing a more personalized experience.

  • To analyze traffic on our website, which helps us understand how users interact with our content and improve overall functionality.

  • To personalize the content and advertisements displayed on the site, ensuring they are relevant to your interests and browsing history.

  • To improve the overall performance of the website, ensuring that it functions efficiently and quickly for an optimal user experience.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the website, such as enabling secure logins and navigating through pages.

  • Performance cookies: These cookies track your interactions with our website, allowing us to gather valuable insights for optimizing content and improving site performance.

  • Functionality cookies: They help the website remember your preferences (e.g., language and location), providing a more tailored experience on your subsequent visits.

  • Targeting cookies: These cookies are used to display ads that are more relevant to your interests, based on your browsing history and online activities.

Your Control Over Cookies:

  • You have the ability to manage or block cookies through the settings on your web browser. Most browsers offer an option to accept or reject cookies, or even delete them altogether.

  • Please note that blocking or disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including limiting your ability to access certain features or services.

  • We recommend keeping cookies enabled to enjoy the full functionality of the site. However, you are free to modify your preferences at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Cookies:

  • In some cases, third-party services, including analytics and advertising providers, may place cookies on your device when you visit our website. These cookies are used to track user behavior and serve targeted ads based on your interests.

  • These third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them to understand how your data is being used.

Updates to Our Cookie Policy:

  • We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or other operational reasons. Any updates will be posted here on this page, with the revised date listed at the top of the policy.

  • We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we are using cookies and to ensure you are aware of any changes.

Contact Us:

  • If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies or this policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
