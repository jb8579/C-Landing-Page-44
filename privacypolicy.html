Privacy Policy for PowerBall

At PowerBall, accessible from powerballaustralia.com, your privacy is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is collected and recorded by PowerBall and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide when using our website or services.

  • We may also collect non-personally identifiable information such as IP addresses, browser types, and cookies that help us analyze traffic and improve our website's functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your information is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services, such as customer support and personalized content.

  • We may use your email address to send you updates, promotions, and information related to our services, unless you opt out.

  • We may also analyze data collected to enhance the functionality and performance of our website, improving your browsing experience.

How We Protect Your Information

  • We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your personal information.

  • However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Sharing Your Information

  • We do not share your personal information with third parties except when required by law or in response to legal requests.

  • We may share non-personally identifiable data with partners and advertisers to help improve our services or deliver targeted content.

Cookies

  • Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. For more information on how we use cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@powerballaustralia.com.

  • You can also opt out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in any email communication.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

  • Any updates will be posted on this page, and the revised date will be indicated at the top of the page.

Contact Us

  • If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@powerballaustralia.com.

  • Alternatively, you can reach us through our website at powerballaustralia.com.
