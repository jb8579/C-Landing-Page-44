Responsible Gambling

Promoting Responsible Gambling

At PowerBall, we are committed to providing a safe, enjoyable, and responsible gambling environment. We encourage all players to gamble responsibly and recognize that gambling should always be a fun activity, not a source of stress or financial hardship. This Responsible Gambling page provides information on how to gamble safely and the tools available to help those who may experience difficulty in controlling their gambling habits.

Understanding Gambling Risks

  • Gambling can be a fun and entertaining activity for many, but it carries risks, including financial loss, emotional distress, and addiction.

  • It's essential to understand that gambling is not a way to make money. The outcomes of all gambling activities are random, and losses are a natural part of the process.

Signs of Problem Gambling

  • It's crucial to recognize the signs of problem gambling. Some indicators include:

  • Spending more money and time on gambling than intended.

  • Chasing losses or gambling more to try to win back money lost.

  • Neglecting other important aspects of life, such as family, work, or social relationships, due to gambling.

  • Feeling anxious, guilty, or upset about gambling habits.

  • Borrowing money or selling personal items to fund gambling activities.

Our Responsible Gambling Tools

  • We provide a variety of tools to help you manage your gambling habits, including:

  • Self-exclusion: You can opt to exclude yourself from gambling on our website for a specified period.

  • Deposit Limits: Set a limit on the amount you can deposit into your account over a specific period to control your spending.

  • Time Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly time limits to control the time spent on our site.

  • Account Closure: If you feel you need to take a break from gambling, you can close your account at any time.

Seeking Help and Support

  • If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, it's important to seek help. There are several organizations that offer support, including:

  • Gambling Help: Call 1800 858 858 or visit www.gamblinghelponline.org.au

  • GambleAware: Visit www.gambleaware.nsw.gov.au

  • BetStop: A national self-exclusion register that allows you to exclude yourself from all Australian online gambling services. Visit www.betstop.gov.au for more details.

PowerBall's Commitment

  • We are committed to providing a safe and responsible environment for our customers. If you ever feel that gambling is no longer a fun experience, please utilize the tools mentioned above or seek help from the organizations listed.

  • PowerBall is fully licensed and regulated by the New South Wales Independent Liquor & Gaming Authority to ensure that all gambling activities are conducted safely and responsibly.

Contact Us

  • If you have any questions or need assistance regarding responsible gambling, feel free to contact us at info@casualfine.com.
